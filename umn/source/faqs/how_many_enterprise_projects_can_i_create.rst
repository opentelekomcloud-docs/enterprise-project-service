How Many Enterprise Projects Can I Create?
==========================================

By default, you can create up to 100 enterprise projects.

If the default quota cannot meet your requirements, you can :ref:`How Do I Apply for a Higher Quota? <em-pm_04_0900__en-us_topic_0040259342_section457218466261>`.

.. note::

   Disabled enterprise projects are still counted towards your enterprise project quota. If your enterprise project quota is insufficient, you can enable the disabled enterprise projects before applying for a higher quota.
