Accessing the Project Management Page
=====================================

Prerequisites
-------------

You have been granted with the permissions to use EPS.

Procedure
---------

#. Log in to the management console.

#. Go to the management console. In the upper right corner, choose **Enterprise Management** from the drop-down list of your account name.

   The **Enterprise Project Management** page is displayed.
